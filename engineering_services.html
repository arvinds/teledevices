TeleDevices, LLC (TDL) has an extensive experience in designing, developing, and testing state-of-the-art Weapons and Communications/Control Systems for electronic warfare, avionics, and ground-based platforms. Under NAICS Code 541330 for Engineering Services, we perform research, analysis, development, design, fabrication, testing, and evaluation of electronic, electromechanical, and mechanical partial and full-scale systems.
