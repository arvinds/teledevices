TDL's professionals provide complete sustainment support for avionics and electronic warfare systems etc. TDL's experienced system sustainment logistics and engineering staff is able to support divergent program requirements. We undertake tasks such as Obsolescence Assessment, Availability Analysis, requirements analysis, evaluation of substitute systems or components, Simulation / Emulation of circuits etc. Our experienced engineers redesign to ensure a perfect "Form, Fit and Function" compatibility as well as long term reliability and maintainability. Additional DMSMS-related services include
  • Logistics services including fielding new systems, field maintenance and assisting units in achieving readiness goals
  • Hardware/software architecture, test, data collection systems and system integration
  • Technical Data Package (TDP) analysis for obsolete materials and outdated specifications
  • Change documentation to update TDPs
  • logistics planning, technical writing, total package fielding, cost analysis, and distribution analysis
