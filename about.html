About Us

Who we are

TeleDevices, LLC (TDL) is an AS9100D and ISO 9001:2015 certified provider of advanced Engineering, Management and Technical Support Services to U.S. Department of Defense.

Our team's excellence in engineering and management is driven by our total commitment to customer satisfaction.

What we do

TeleDevices provides high-quality engineering services and technical support to the U.S. Department of Defense. TDL's outstanding engineers and researchers are often tasked with finding solutions, on time and within budget, to complex problems.

We have deep expertise in design/prototyping services for form-fit-functional replacement of Electronic Warfare, Common Avionics, and Communications systems. TDL has engineering expertise in Digital/Analog/RF circuit design, DMSMS solutions, Reverse Engineering, Power Supplies, System Integration, Test Engineering, Software/ OFP development.
