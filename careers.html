We're Hiring Engineers!

TeleDevices, LLC (TDL) is an advanced engineering solutions firm. We are looking for Electrical Engineers with design experience in RF analog circuit design OR digital embedded control / FPGA design. Familiarity with military aircraft Communications, Navigation & Surveillance systems a plus.

We are a team of self-starters who take pride in our work. We offer a great work environment with exciting technical opportunities.

Requirements

BS EE ( MS EE preferred) degree with 5 years of hands-on RF Analog Circuit Design or Digital circuit design experience is required.

Expertise with EDA tools for schematic capture, simulation etc., in-depth knowledge of the fundamentals of RF circuit design (RF Amp, Filter, PLL etc.) and for digital designers real time operating systems, micro-controllers, C/C++, VHDL, FPGA design is desirable.

Must be capable of capturing legacy system design and implementing new hardware design to support system sustainment. Familiarity with the military documentation requirements a plus. LabView experience for test development is advantageous.

Applicants selected may be subjected to a government security investigation and must meet eligibility requirements for access to classified information.
